Gelatina de Mama Carmen

Gelatina

Gelatina Recipe by Mama Carmen. Milk based.

Ingredients

  • 6 packets Knox unflavored gelatin
  • 8 cups of milk
  • 2 1/2 cans 14 oz lechera (2 to reduce sweetness)
  • 1 spoonful vanilla extract
  • 4 oz strawberry Jello
  • 4 oz orange Jello
  • 4 oz lime Jello
  • 4 oz lemon Jello

Steps

Jello Prep

  • Repeat steps for each Jello flavor
  1. Boil 3/4 cup water
  2. Stir boiled water in a bowl with powdered Jello until dissolved
  3. Add 3/4 cup cold water and stir
  4. Leave in fridge for 4 hrs to cool

Main Steps

  1. In a pot, mix milk and the 6 unflavored Knox packets
  2. Add lechera and vanilla and stir until smooth
  3. Place pot on heat and stir until boiling
  4. Turn off heat until not boiling, then turn heat back on until boiling again, stir continuously
  5. Turn off heat and stir until cool enough to pour
  6. Use a colander to strain into a glass baking mold. Stir every once in a while until fully cooled
  7. Cut flavored Jello into squares and place squares into the mold
  8. Place into fridge to solidify overnight
